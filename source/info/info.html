What is Purify all about?

Every time we ask Google to search for something requests are sent back and forth. Internet Service Providers need electricity to keep the internet running and Google needs electricity for their servers.

1) Careful estimates believe that each Google search amounts to at least 0.3 Wh, which in turn amounts to no less than 0.2 g of CO 2 . [source]

2) The most common searches on Google are aimed at popular web services, like facebook, YouTube, and even Google itself. These trivial searches can be prevented. [source]

Purify cuts down your carbon footprint by preventing unneccessary Google searches!

Every time you use Google Search to access your favourite website, Purify stops the superfluous web request and instead opens the page directly for you - while reducing your CO 2 emissions and also saving you time!
